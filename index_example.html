        Polytropos--The Journey of a Reader

        This book collects a large portion of the English book reviews written by a high school student between 2022 and 2025. Together, they trace the evolution of his thought, taste, and identity. Arranged in reverse chronological order—from the most recent to the earliest—they retrace the path of a boy becoming who he is today. This book is currently published on Amazon Kindle.
